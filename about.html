About This System

The Plant Disease Detection System is an intelligent web-based application that helps identify plant diseases by analyzing leaf images. The system is designed to assist farmers and agriculture students in detecting diseases at an early stage.

Users can simply upload an image of a plant leaf, and the system automatically analyzes the image using a trained deep learning model. Based on the visual features of the leaf, the system predicts whether the plant is healthy or affected by a disease.

Along with disease detection, the system also provides basic treatment recommendations. This helps users take timely action to prevent further crop damage and improve plant health.
